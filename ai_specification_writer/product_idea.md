# Product Idea

## Vision
Enable companies to reduce transportation costs and environmental impact by helping employees share their daily commutes through intelligent carpool matching.

## Users
- **Corporate Employees**: want affordable, convenient, and reliable commuting options with colleagues who have similar routes and schedules.
- **HR Managers**: want to improve employee satisfaction while tracking cost savings and sustainability impact from carpool participation.

## Key Features
- **Smart Commute Matching** – Matches employees with similar routes and schedules using an intelligent algorithm.
- **Corporate SSO Integration** – Secure login through company identity systems for seamless employee access.
- **Environmental Impact Tracking** – Calculates and reports CO₂ savings from shared commuting.
- **Cost Savings Analytics** – Shows employees and companies how much money is saved through carpooling.
- **Schedule Coordination** – In-app messaging and notifications for organizing and managing carpool schedules.
