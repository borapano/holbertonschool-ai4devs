# Product Idea

## Vision
Enable companies to reduce transportation costs and environmental impact by helping employees share their daily commutes through intelligent carpool matching.

## Users
- **Corporate Employees**: want affordable, convenient, and social commuting options with colleagues who have similar routes and schedules.  
- **HR Managers**: want to improve employee satisfaction while tracking benefits such as cost savings and participation in sustainability initiatives.  
- **Facility Managers**: want to reduce parking demand and optimize the use of available parking spaces.  
- **Sustainability Officers**: want to measure and report reductions in carbon emissions from employee commuting.

## Key Features
- Smart commute matching based on routes, schedules, and preferences  
- Corporate SSO integration for secure and seamless employee access  
- Environmental impact tracking with CO₂ savings reports  
- Cost savings analytics for employees and companies  
- Parking optimization with reserved spots for carpool participants  
- In-app messaging and schedule coordination for carpool groups
